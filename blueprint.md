# **App Name**: Trichology AI Assistant

## Core Features:

- Informational Website: Develop a simple website presenting general information about the clinic, services, contact information, and a couple of educational blog entries related to trichology.
- WhatsApp Integration: Integrate a WhatsApp Business account for automated communication.
- Pre-Consultation FAQs: Automated responses to frequently asked questions, such as clinic hours, location, and required information prior to the consultation.
- Post-Consultation Support: Automated assistance for post-procedure care, medication reminders, and follow-up appointment scheduling.
- Smart Scheduling: An intelligent appointment booking system providing guidance throughout the process. Send confirmations and reminders using the tool for a naturalistic feel.
- Personalized AI Responses: Customize the AI's responses and conversation style to reflect Dr. Sara's personality and medical protocols using a tool, providing patients with tailored advice and support.
- Lead Generation: Capture structured data of potential patients to enhance the quality of leads and pre-qualify patients effectively.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Neural blue (#2563EB) to evoke trust and medical professionalism.
- Background color: Light gray (#F8FAFC), nearly desaturated neural blue, for a clean and professional backdrop.
- Accent color: Slightly darker blue (#4F46E5), an analogous color, for highlights and calls to action.
- Body and headline font: 'Outfit' (sans-serif) for a modern and clear presentation of medical content.
- Use clear and professional icons, primarily related to medicine and care, with the neural blue as the primary color.
- A clean, structured layout that prioritizes readability and easy access to essential information.
- Subtle transitions for button hovering feedback.
